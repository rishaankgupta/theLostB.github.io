I'm a 16-year-old polymath from India with an insatiable curiosity for understanding how things work — from the quantum mechanics of reality to the philosophy of consciousness.

This blog, The Midnight Editor, is my digital garden where I cultivate ideas at the intersection of technology, science, philosophy, and creativity. Every piece I write is an attempt to distill complex concepts into clear, actionable insights.

I believe in learning deeply, thinking independently, and creating relentlessly. My goal is to become a polymath who bridges disciplines — someone who can write code, compose music, understand physics, and debate philosophy with equal facility.

  • Science — Physics, mathematics, neuroscience, and complexity
  • Philosophy — Epistemology, existentialism, and mental models
  • Creativity — Design, music, writing, and art
